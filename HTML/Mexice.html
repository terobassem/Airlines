San Francisco Leon Hotel

This hotel offers 133 rooms located on 4 floors, which can be reached by lift. The hospitable staff at the reception are happy to assist with any inquiries. Equipping the building includes storing luggage and a safe. The property has access to WiFi without paying an additional fee. Wheelchair friendly facilities are available. The hotel's outdoor facilities include a beautiful garden and a children's playground. Guests arriving with their own cars can park them without paying an additional fee in the building's own indoor garage. Additional services include room service, a laundry service, a coin-operated washing machine and a receptionist service.

Riu Plaza Guadalajara Hotel

This hotel offers 550 rooms, including 260 single rooms and 140 double rooms, distributed on 42 floors and can be reached by lift. The multilingual staff is happy to provide advice and assistance to guests at all times in the reception lobby, and check-in and check-out are possible 24 hours a day. Facilities and services at the hotel include luggage storage, a safe and an ATM. Via Wi-Fi guests have access to the Internet. Assistance with booking trips and excursions can be obtained at the tour desk. This hotel offers a range of amenities suitable for people with special needs. Wheelchair friendly facilities are available. A supermarket, souvenir shop and other shops can be used for shopping and strolling. In addition, the garden provides a suitable space for relaxation and outdoor recreation. Guests arriving by car can park them in the indoor garage or in the outdoor garage. Additional services include 24-hour security, car rental, medical assistance, room service, laundry service, hairdresser, coin-operated washing machine and private shuttle bus. There are daily newspapers (use is subject to a fee). The business center is happy to assist with businesses and also provides a fax machine.
